Hi there! My name is María Amantani Reynoso, but as you can probably guess, I go by Ami (you know, for short). I'm 23 years old, and I currently work as a front-end developer in a really cool advertising agency.

I started venturing on the field when I was around 12, by building mini websites called fanlistings as a hobby. That's how I taught myself HTML and CSS, and learned how to use tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator. I also grasped some pretty good notions of PHP and MySQL.

Well, but that's just how I started. At University I crossed paths with so many different technologies and areas (such as cinematography or photography, among others), that formed me as a whole Image and Sound Designer.

What I most enjoy about my work is translating visual experiences into code, always by exploring new horizons and pushing myself to deliver my best.
