Hi there! My name is María Amantani Reynoso, but as you can probably guess, I go by Ami (you know, for short). I'm 25 years old, and I currently work as a Sr Front End Engineer at Mercado Libre, the top e-commerce platform in Latin America.

I am currently transitioning into a full stack developer. Also, I am a speaker! On 2016 I spoke at 2 international conferences.

Well, but that's just how I started. At University I crossed paths with so many different technologies and areas (such as cinematography or photography, among others), that formed me as a whole Image and Sound Designer.

What I most enjoy about my work is translating visual experiences into code, always by exploring new horizons and pushing myself to deliver my best.
